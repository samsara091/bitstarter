What if you could create a map of your life? A map that contains photos, videos, blogs, and stories all tied to locations and actual dates within their life? This application enables you to trace the path of their childhood.

Based on Google maps, you can now track your daily routines and activities in a geographical manner. Starting from their usual hangouts, such as parks, friends houses, the family house, movie theater, day care, and more you can add photos, videos, and blogs for each day of the week. We combine a calendar, map, scrapbook, and video archive into one single source of history.

This app makes it easy to quickly add your media (photos, video, audio, and blogs) from your phone, tablet, or desktop. The application will automatically include your location and the date to ensure you never forget it. No longer do you need to upload to Facebook, Pinterest, or Google+ to ensure you remember the date and time of the event. Now you just do it once on our app and the memory is saved forever. Further, if you still would like to share it with friends and family, our application effortlessly syncs with Twitter, Facebook, Google+ and more.

Our application also has a built in calendar feature to help you remember your appointments. You can leverage existing calendars from gmail, outlook, yahoo, and mac.

In addition to uploading your own media, you can also leverage our own colorful artwork, sound effects, animation, photo effects, and video editing capabilities.

As your child grows, this app grows with them. Once they have their own phone, tablet, or laptop you can have them reflect on their own events and lives. The app enables your child to craft blog entries, speak to the app like a diary / journal, and add their own audio and video. Our app is so easy to use, children can focus on the event and their own recollections rather than learning a complicated user interface.

As the days go by, our application allows you to quickly export spans of time. You can select a single day, or a range of dates and then easily create a journal. Further, if you have more than one user in your family, you can create albums covering everyone. Want to remember what your entire family was doing during the holiday season of 2014? A few quick clicks and ll have an album of photos, blogs, tweets, facebook posts, videos, and texts all in one.

New features will be added at time they are appeared. Map Scrapbook also sets itself apart with its effortless syncing via iCloud that works nearly error-free between iOS devices and the companion Mac app.

Some key features:
  • Instant photo diary of one or more of your family members
  • Create albums spanning any interval of time. For example, you can create an album covering the last 10 months but only the weekends. Or the last 6 years of Christmas. Or the last 5 summer vacations.
  • Photo calendar view to easily check out your child's growth
  • Keep your photos shared with only those who you select
This App is recommended for parents raising children who:
  • want to keep diary easily to keep record of kid's growth
  • add memories in word to each photo of kids
  • can't find the time to send my parents the photos of my child right after I take them
  • find trouble organizing kids' photos
  • are busy working
  • want to watch over the growth and the life of my child, even though I am home late on business days
  • are hesitant to share kids' photos on blogs
  • love cute and helpful apps
  • want to send pictures to relatives who don't have a smartphone, so they want photos to be sent to their PC
